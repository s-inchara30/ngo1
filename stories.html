Happy Tails

Every adoption is a success story. Here are some of our favorites.

Rocky's Second Chance

Rocky was found abandoned with a severe leg injury. After months of rehabilitation and surgery, he was adopted by the Miller family. "He's brought so much joy to our lives," says Mrs. Miller.

Bella Finds Her Forever Home

Bella spent 2 years in our shelter before being adopted by a retired teacher. "I can't imagine life without her now," says her new owner. They go for daily walks and even visit nursing homes together.

Max and His Best Friend

Max was a shy, fearful dog when he came to us. After patient training, he was adopted by a family with an autistic son. They've formed an incredible bond that's helped both of them grow.
